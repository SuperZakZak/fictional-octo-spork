# Shopping Bag Configurator Mockups

This folder should contain mockup images for the Shopping Bag configurator in different colors.

## Required Images

Place images with the following names (AVIF format):

- `black-1.avif` - Shopping Bag mockup in black color
- `grey.avif` - Shopping Bag mockup in grey color
- `midnight-blue.avif` - Shopping Bag mockup in midnight blue color (#3d4f6b)
- `natural.avif` - Shopping Bag mockup in natural/off-white color (#f8f4e8)
- `white-1.avif` - Shopping Bag mockup in white color
- `white-2.avif` - Shopping Bag mockup in white color (alternative)

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG (for transparency), AVIF, WebP, or JPG
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 (square)
- **Recommended Size**: 1000x1000px
- **Background**: Transparent or white
- **Design Area**: Clear space in the center for user designs
- **Quality**: High resolution for configurator preview

## Color Codes

- Black: `#000000` ‚Üí `black-1.avif`
- Grey: `#d1d1d1` ‚Üí `grey.avif`
- Midnight Blue: `#3d4f6b` ‚Üí `midnight-blue.avif`
- Natural: `#f8f4e8` ‚Üí `natural.avif`
- White: `#FFFFFF` ‚Üí `white-1.avif` or `white-2.avif`
